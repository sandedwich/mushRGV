THE ANALOG COLLECTION

The Analog Collection is four high-quality LP records of randomly selected musical compositions from various artists, across various genres, stemming throughout various eras in music history. Each collection is assembled entirely from a library Choke has built from the ground up, and it includes albums, EPs, singles, compilations, as well as remixes.
Included is...
  • 4 LPs
  • Needle dropped versions of each record
  • Notes on the music from our teams' listening sessions
  • Shipping & Handling
