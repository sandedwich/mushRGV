How the music in your order is selected

The list of genres provided will be the only factor in determining the content of an individual's order. Choke will pick from within the specified list and compile a collection ready for shipping. Your privacy will be kept.

Delivery Time

Assembly and Packaging: 2-5 days
Shipping: 2-8 days

Estimated Delivery Time: ~12 days.

Note: Choke will delivery directly should the delivery address be within a 15 mile radius of its office location in Mercedes, Tx.

New or Used?

Completely assembled through second-hand sources. Used discs, used record, used tape. Everything that leaves the facility is guaranteed to meet this standard of quality.

What Choke looks for when adding to its catalog

It's all very subjective, but anything that sounds good and gives the impression that it was driven by creative values instead of commercial ones.

Not profit-seeking but charge $20?
Choke excersises a "profit-second" model of transaction. Little emphasis is placed on revenue. $20 covers expenses with enough left over to reinvest into itself. A detailed breakdown of its resource allocation is made available for anyone to see.
