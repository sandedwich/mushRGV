Jetwad

the idea first came onto them by guitarist ethan nowlin. along with eduardo trevino, they set out to find their third member. luckily, ed had a friend, aaron perez, who had been playing percussion for a few years. the three joined up to form the first iteration of jetwad. in august 2017, the 3 planned a show in ed's backyard inviting quite literally everyone in their high school marching band.

early that following year, jetwad's line-up consisted of ethan, ed, and new drummer frank hernandez. jetwad didn't offically come onto the scene until the summer of 2018. their debut show, hosted by patrick over at TIGERSBLOOD, saw acts from good ghosts, glare, and the then touring band, tears on tape. jetwad's (not so) historic set can be found over at theshark15z's channel.

following several shows throughout the second half of 2018, jetwad released "roger, roger!". the ep consisted of 5 songs, 3 of which had been written solely by frank herndandez some time before joining jetwad. 1 was written by ethan and ed, and 1 was written by ethan and frank.

in march of 2019, the trio hosted their first show, dubbing it "jetfest". it saw acts from astral lotus, bison, the kleptos, the vennz and about 10 minutes of the vonn shweetz before mercedes pd shut the event down. jetfest is a staple in their history as it was the culminating event of days-worth of planning, great music, and witnessing the community come together.

the rest of 2019 was spent giging around they valley. in december, jetwad released copies of "...and it too" to their crowd during a show at the historic cine el rey. the work consisted of unfinished, unpolished, and raw demos.
